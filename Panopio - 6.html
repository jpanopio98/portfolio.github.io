Mountain and Garden

This project is the second in my series of Sumi-e style works. I wanted to mimic this style of painting digitally. For this particular piece I compiled images of the sky, mountains and a garden and edited them through Photoshop to create a blended environment effect. I was really inspired by landscape paintings and wanted to try out new tools in Photoshop that made this piece possible.
