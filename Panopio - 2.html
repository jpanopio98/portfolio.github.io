Starry Great Waves

Hokusai's Great Waves is one of my favorite art pieces of all time so I decided to remaster it in GIF format to give the illusion of the waves moving. I also overlay an image of the stars on the waves. I edited the filter to make it so that only blue is emphasized greatly and the rest are colored gray.
