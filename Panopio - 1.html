Eye of the Flower

This piece is a simple conceptual art piece. The concept I had in mind while making this piece was "beauty is in the eye of the beholder." I had edited the image so only the lips of the woman were emphasized and beautiful flowers were put across her face to symbolize a kind of beauty.
