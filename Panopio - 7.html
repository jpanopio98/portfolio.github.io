Light and Dark

This was my very first digital media art piece. My concept for this piece was to take an image of Anakin Skywalker and Darth Vader and overlay them on top of each other to symbolize Anakin's struggle between light and dark. I wanted this piece to emphasize my love for this character in pop culture.
